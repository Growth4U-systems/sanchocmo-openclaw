## Core Aesthetic

Must look like a SCANNED PAGE from a comic printed on cheap newsprint 50 years ago.
If it looks digital, clean, or modern — it's WRONG.

### Rendering Technique
- Ink: Thick black (#000000) outlines with brush weight variation (thick contours, thin details)
- Shading: VISIBLE Ben-Day halftone dots — not subtle, not smooth gradients
- Shadows: Dense black chiaroscuro, never soft
- Print imperfections: Color bleed outside lines, CMYK misregistration, paper grain fibers, ink absorption (desaturated colors), edge wear

### Color Palette (CMYK Vintage — always desaturated)

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Paper | #F5F0E6 | Background, skin, eyes (NEVER pure white) |
| Terracotta | #E66E40 | Sancho's vest accent, logo, energy |
| Navy | #1E3A5F | Quijote armor, cold shadows, villains |
| Retro Cyan | #00AEEF | AI rays, holograms, sky (always with halftone dots) |
| Alert Red | #FF0000 | Onomatopoeias, danger (use sparingly) |
| Ink Black | #000000 | Outlines, text, hard shadows |
| Earth Brown | #8B6914 | Landscape, wood, donkey |
| Olive | #4A5D23 | Dry vegetation (minimal — La Mancha is arid) |

CRITICAL: Colors must look AGED and ABSORBED by cheap paper. Never fresh/saturated/digital.

## Character Models

### Sancho Panza (PRIMARY — the SanchoCMO mascot)

Study `assets/reference-sanchocmo2.jpg` carefully for the canonical look:

- **Build**: Short, stocky, solid (not exaggeratedly fat — sturdy peasant)
- **Face**: Round, tanned Mediterranean skin, expressive thick eyebrows
- **Beard**: Short-cropped dark brown bushy beard (NOT long, NOT black)
- **Hat**: Soft brown flat-brimmed Spanish campesino felt hat (NOT cowboy, NOT medieval helmet)
- **Shirt**: White/cream button shirt, sleeves rolled to elbows showing hairy forearms
- **Vest**: Muted brownish-khaki wool vest (jubón) buttoned up — in the reference it reads as a warm earth tone, NOT bright orange
- **S Patch**: Dark square "S" logo FABRIC PATCH hand-sewn on chest with VISIBLE thick stitches. Must look worn.
- **Pants**: Brown baggy breeches (calzones bombachos)
- **Footwear**: Leather gaiters/boots
- **Belt**: Thick leather belt, sometimes with tools hanging
- **Mount**: Small faithful grey donkey (Rucio) with saddlebags
- **Tech**: When holding technology (tablet, device), it should look ANOMALOUS in peasant hands — the contrast IS the humor

Typical expressions: alarm (mouth open, wide eyes), determination, practical concern, confident grin

### Don Quijote (secondary character)

- **Build**: Tall, skeletal, extremely thin
- **Face**: Angular, grey pointed beard, wild eyes (sometimes white without pupils)
- **Helmet**: YELMO DE BACIA (barber's brass basin with half-moon cutout) — NEVER a closed-visor medieval helmet
- **Armor**: Full 17th-century plate armor — MUST be dented, rusted, dirty. Never shiny or fantasy.
- **Weapon**: Long lance (sometimes used as antenna/pointer)
- **S Logo**: ENGRAVED (chiseled) into chest plate metal with rust in the grooves
- **Mount**: Rocinante — a thin, scraggly horse

## World: "La Mancha Digital"

Two eras colliding visually:

**17th Century Layer**: Arid La Mancha plains, cracked earth, dusty roads, windmills, wooden inns, harsh midday sun or dramatic orange sunsets

**Retro-Futuristic Tech Layer** (technology is NEVER modern/clean):
- Windmill-Bots: Windmills transforming into robots with CRT monitor faces
- Monsters: Robots made of old TVs, mainframes, satellite dishes, giant floppy disks
- AI Magic: Retro laser beams (Kirby dots/crackle) from rustic tools
- Ground debris: Floppy disks, CDs, cables, ball mice scattered on desert floor
- Social icons: Physical objects (stone thumbs-up, mechanical Twitter birds)
- Clouds: Tangled cables and floating rusty servers

## Comic Panel Elements

- **Borders**: Thick black panel borders (3-5px)
- **Caption boxes**: Yellow/cream rectangles with narrative italic text
- **Speech bubbles**: Hand-drawn imperfect borders, cream background, ALL CAPS text
- **Onomatopoeias**: GIANT blocky 3D letters with halftone fills (CLICK!, ZAP!, ERROR 404!, ALGORITMO!)
- **Background**: ALWAYS detailed — never empty/flat

## Detail Level (CRITICAL)

Images must be COMPLEX and rich:
- Multiple elements per scene (characters + environment + tech + text elements)
- Textures on ALL surfaces (rust on metal, grain on fabric, cracks in earth)
- Detailed and legible FACIAL EXPRESSIONS
- Clothing with folds, wrinkles, visible wear
- Backgrounds ALWAYS populated with environmental detail

## NEVER Include
- 3D/CGI/Pixar look
- Modern clothing (sneakers, jeans, hoodies)
- Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Smooth gradients
- Perfect symmetry
- Empty/flat backgrounds
- Simplified/icon/flat-design illustration
- Fresh/saturated colors
- Uniform line weights
- Generic character designs that don't match the models above
