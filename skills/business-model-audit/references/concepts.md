# Business Model Audit — Conceptos y Metodología

---

## Revenue Model Archetypes

| Model | Key Metric | Natural Motion | Primary Channel | Warning Sign |
|-------|-----------|----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Subscription (ACV < 5K) | Activation rate | PLG pure | SEO + directories + trials | Payback > 12mo |
| Subscription (ACV 5-50K) | PQL rate | PLG-to-Sales hybrid | Content + LinkedIn + demos | CAC > 30% of ACV |
| Subscription (ACV 50K+) | Pipeline coverage | Sales-Led + ABM | Events + analysts + exec outreach | Sales cycle > 9mo |
| Transaction/Pay-per-use | First txn rate, repeat | Performance + embedded | SEM transactional + API docs | CAC vs 1st txn (usar cohort LTV) |
| Marketplace | Liquidity ratio | Supply-first + demand SEM | Outbound (supply) + SEO (demand) | Paid demand before liquidity |
| Freemium | Free-to-paid % | PLG activation + viral | SEO volume + virality | High signups low activation = product gap |
| Usage-based | NRR, expansion revenue | Land-and-expand + DevRel | Developer content + community | NRR < 100% = shrinking base |
| Hybrid (sub + usage) | NDR, upsell rate | Segmented: SMB=PLG, ENT=ABM | By segment | Floor too low = unprofitable |

## Growth Motion Classification

| Signal | PLG | MLG | Sales-Led |
|--------|-----|-----|-----------|
| Pricing visible | Yes, self-serve | Yes + "talk to sales" | Hidden / "contact us" |
| Trial/free tier | Yes | Lead magnet / gated | Demo only |
| ACV | < 5K EUR/year | 5-50K EUR/year | > 50K EUR/year |
| Decision maker | Individual user | Manager / Ops | C-suite / committee |
| Sales cycle | Days-weeks | Weeks-months | Months-quarters |
| Best channels | SEO, directories, viral | Content, LinkedIn, webinars | ABM, events, analysts |
| Content type | Templates, tools, tutorials | Case studies, ROI calculators | Business cases, compliance |

## FinTech-specific

- Regulatory marketing constraints (MiFID II, CNMV crypto, GDPR targeting)
- Trust signals as table stakes (licenses, certifications, volume)
- Compliance review adds 2-4 weeks to campaigns
- LinkedIn + sector events + PR = primary (not social ads)

---

## Lite vs Deep Criteria

**Lite done**: B2B/B2C classified, revenue model identified, acquisition sources listed, PLG/MLG/Sales-led assessed, Discovery Tasks logged

**Deep done**: + unit economics (LTV, CAC, payback), full funnel mapped with conversions, sector benchmarks compared, growth motion comparison vs top 3 competitors, expansion revenue assessed, model-motion fit validated

## Edge Cases

### Pre-revenue
- Mapear modelo intencionado y assumptions
- Benchmark contra sector averages
- "No tenéis ingresos todavía — mapeemos el modelo que tenéis en mente."

### Multiple revenue models
- Capturar primario (>60% revenue) + secundario
- "¿Cuál genera la mayor parte de los ingresos hoy?"

### No funnel exists
- Documentar: "Funnel: none. Acquisition: informal/word-of-mouth."
- Phase 2 empieza de cero

### Client thinks they're PLG but they're not
- Si ACV > 10K y no self-serve → son MLG o Sales-led
- "Vuestro ticket y proceso sugieren ventas asistidas. PLG viable si reducís barrera de entrada."
