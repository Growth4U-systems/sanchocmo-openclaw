## Principles

1. **Evidence > Opinions** — What they DO > what they SAY they'll do.
2. **Fast Validation** — 7-14 days per ECP max.
3. **Cheap Tests First** — Interviews (free) → LP (€50) → MVI (€200) → Full MVP (€5K+).
4. **Fail Fast** — Goal is to INVALIDATE bad ECPs quickly.
5. **Minimum Viable Evidence** — 15-20 interviews + 100 waitlist signups = enough.
