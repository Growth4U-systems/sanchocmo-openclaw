# Content Mix Templates

> Reference data for content-calendar-planner skill. Templates for different business types showing pillar structure, format distribution, funnel weights, and frequency benchmarks.

---

## Template Selection Guide

| Business Type | Primary Channels | Content Velocity | Key Pillar Focus |
|---------------|-----------------|------------------|------------------|
| B2B SaaS | Blog, LinkedIn, Newsletter | Medium (8-12 pieces/month) | Product education + Thought leadership |
| Fintech | Blog, LinkedIn, Email | Medium-Low (6-10 pieces/month) | Trust/compliance + Education |
| E-commerce D2C | Instagram, TikTok, Email | High (20-30 pieces/month) | Visual + UGC + Promotions |
| Agency/Consultancy | LinkedIn, Blog, Newsletter | Medium (8-12 pieces/month) | Authority + Case studies |
| Healthcare/Clinics | Blog, Social, Email | Low-Medium (6-8 pieces/month) | Trust + Education + Testimonials |
| Marketplace | SEO Blog, Social (both sides) | High (15-20 pieces/month) | Supply + Demand balance |
| Pre-launch | LinkedIn (personal), Blog | Low-Medium (5-8 pieces/month) | Audience building + Problem awareness |

## B2B SaaS Template

**Typical channels**: Blog (2x/month), LinkedIn (3-5x/week), Newsletter (1x/week), Twitter (3-5x/week)

### Content Pillars (3-5)

| Pillar | Description | Funnel Stage | % of Content |
|--------|-------------|--------------|--------------|
| Product Education | How-to guides, tutorials, feature deep-dives | MOFU | 25% |
| Thought Leadership | Industry trends, opinions, predictions | TOFU | 30% |
| Customer Success | Case studies, testimonials, ROI stories | BOFU | 20% |
| Industry Insights | Market analysis, benchmarks, research | TOFU | 15% |
| Behind the Scenes | Team stories, product updates, culture | TOFU | 10% |

### Format Distribution

| Format | Channel | Frequency | Pillar Alignment |
|--------|---------|-----------|-----------------|
| Long-form article | Blog | 2x/month | Product Education, Industry Insights |
| LinkedIn post | LinkedIn | 3x/week | Thought Leadership, Behind the Scenes |
| LinkedIn article | LinkedIn | 1x/month | Thought Leadership |
| Newsletter edition | Email | 1x/week | Mixed (curated + original) |
| Twitter thread | Twitter | 2x/week | Thought Leadership, Industry Insights |
| Case study | Blog + LinkedIn | 1x/month | Customer Success |
| Video (short) | LinkedIn, Twitter | 2x/month | Product Education, Behind the Scenes |

### Funnel Distribution

- **TOFU (55%)**: Thought leadership, industry insights, behind the scenes
- **MOFU (25%)**: Product education, comparison guides, webinar recaps
- **BOFU (20%)**: Case studies, ROI calculators, demo CTAs

## Weekly Rhythm Patterns

### Pattern A: Theme Weeks (rotate monthly)

```
Week 1: Pillar 1 focus (e.g., Education)
Week 2: Pillar 2 focus (e.g., Case Studies)
Week 3: Pillar 3 focus (e.g., Industry Analysis)
Week 4: Mixed / Experimental
```

### Pattern B: Fixed Format Days

```
Monday:    Long-form (blog post or LinkedIn article)
Tuesday:   Visual (carousel, infographic, video)
Wednesday: Engagement (question, poll, thread)
Thursday:  Authority (insight, opinion, data)
Friday:    Light (behind the scenes, personal, recap)
```

### Pattern C: Funnel Rotation

```
Week 1: TOFU heavy (awareness, education)
Week 2: MOFU heavy (comparison, how-to)
Week 3: BOFU heavy (proof, CTA)
Week 4: TOFU + experiment
```

## Cadence Quick Reference

| Team Size | Realistic Monthly Output | Focus |
|-----------|------------------------|-------|
| Founder solo (5-10h/week) | 8-12 pieces | 1-2 channels max, personal brand |
| Founder + 1 marketer (15-20h/week) | 15-25 pieces | 2-3 channels, mix of formats |
| Small team (3-5 people) | 30-50 pieces | 3-4 channels, full format mix |
| Full marketing team | 50+ pieces | All relevant channels, experimentation |

**Rule of thumb**: Better to post consistently on 2 channels than inconsistently on 5.
