# Channel Scoring Matrix

> Reference data for channel-prioritization skill. Scoring rubrics for 9 marketing channel types across 5 dimensions.

---

## Scoring Dimensions (1-10 scale)

| # | Dimension | Weight | What It Measures |
|---|-----------|--------|------------------|
| 1 | ICP-fit | 0.25 | How well does this channel reach YOUR specific ECP? |
| 2 | Budget-fit | 0.20 | Can the client's budget support this channel meaningfully? |
| 3 | Team-capacity | 0.20 | Does the team have the skills AND hours to execute? |
| 4 | Competitive-gap | 0.15 | Are competitors underutilizing this channel? |
| 5 | Time-to-first-result | 0.20 | How fast can this channel produce measurable output? |

**Formula**: `Score = (ICP × 0.25) + (Budget × 0.20) + (Team × 0.20) + (Gap × 0.15) + (Time × 0.20)`

**Threshold**: Score >= 6.0 = recommended. Score 4.0-5.9 = conditional. Score < 4.0 = skip.

## Channel Profiles

### 1. Content / SEO (Blog, guides, pillar pages)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-Many, Organic (Warm)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: ICP searches for solutions online. 5-7: Some search intent. 1-4: ICP doesn't search (elderly, offline industries) |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: < EUR 500/mo needed (writer time). 5-7: Need tools (Ahrefs EUR 99/mo). 1-4: Need agency (EUR 2K+/mo) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Someone can write 2+ articles/month. 5-7: 1 article/month possible. 1-4: No writer, no time |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors have weak/no blog. 5-7: Average competition. 1-4: Saturated SERPs, DR 70+ competitors |
| Time-to-first-result | 3-5 typical. New domain = 3-6 months to rank. Existing domain with authority = 1-2 months. |

**Best for**: B2B SaaS, Professional Services, Fintech, Healthcare, Education
**Budget range**: EUR 0 (founder writes) to EUR 5K/mo (agency + tools)
**Team requirement**: 5-10h/week minimum for consistent publishing
**Tools**: WordPress/Ghost, Ahrefs/SEMrush, GSC (free), Surfer SEO

### 2. Social Organic (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-Many, Organic (Warm)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: ICP active on platform daily. 5-7: Present but passive. 1-4: ICP not on social |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: Free (founder posts). 5-7: Need design tools (EUR 50/mo). 1-4: Need social manager (EUR 1.5K+/mo) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Founder/team member enjoys creating content. 5-7: Can manage 3x/week. 1-4: No one wants to post |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors silent on platform. 5-7: Average presence. 1-4: Competitors dominate feed |
| Time-to-first-result | 5-7 typical. First engagement in days. Meaningful audience in 2-3 months of consistent posting. |

**Platform selection guide**:
- LinkedIn: B2B decision makers, professional services, SaaS, fintech
- Twitter/X: Tech, crypto, startup ecosystem, developer tools
- Instagram: D2C, lifestyle, food, fashion, healthcare (visual)
- TikTok: Gen Z/Millennial audiences, education, entertainment, D2C

**Best for**: Personal brand-led companies, B2B (LinkedIn), D2C (Instagram/TikTok)
**Budget range**: EUR 0 to EUR 2K/mo
**Team requirement**: 3-5h/week per platform
**Tools**: Buffer, Hootsuite, Metricool, Canva, native scheduling

### 3. Email Marketing (Newsletter, nurture sequences)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-Many, Organic (Warm)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: ICP checks email regularly (professionals). 5-7: Mixed. 1-4: ICP avoids email (Gen Z, certain B2C) |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: Free tier covers needs (< 1K subs). 5-7: EUR 50-200/mo. 1-4: Enterprise ESP needed (EUR 500+/mo) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Can write weekly newsletter. 5-7: Bi-weekly possible. 1-4: No email writing capacity |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors don't email. 5-7: Standard newsletters. 1-4: Competitors have strong email game |
| Time-to-first-result | 6-8 typical. Immediate results once you have a list. Building list = depends on lead magnet + traffic. |

**Requires**: Existing list OR lead magnet + traffic source to build one
**Best for**: SaaS, Professional Services, E-commerce, Education, Media
**Budget range**: EUR 0 (free tiers) to EUR 500/mo
**Team requirement**: 2-4h/week
**Tools**: Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, Brevo, Beehiiv

### 4. Cold Outreach (Email + LinkedIn prospecting)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-One, Organic (Cold)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: Clear decision maker with professional email/LinkedIn. 5-7: Reachable but fragmented. 1-4: No clear contact point |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: EUR 100-300/mo (tools). 5-7: EUR 300-1K/mo (tools + data). 1-4: Need SDR team (EUR 3K+/mo) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Someone can dedicate 5h+/week to outreach. 5-7: 2-3h/week. 1-4: No one available for outreach |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors don't do outbound. 5-7: Standard outreach. 1-4: ICP inbox saturated |
| Time-to-first-result | 7-9 typical. First meetings in 2-4 weeks. Pipeline building = 1-3 months. |

**Best for**: B2B with high ACV (EUR 5K+/year), Professional Services, Consulting, Enterprise SaaS
**Budget range**: EUR 100 (DIY) to EUR 5K/mo (tools + VA/SDR)
**Team requirement**: 5-10h/week
**Tools**: Instantly, Lemlist, Apollo, Expandi, LinkedIn Sales Navigator

### 5. Paid Ads (Meta, Google, LinkedIn Ads)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-Many, Paid (Cold or Warm retargeting)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: Strong search intent (Google) or targetable demographics (Meta/LinkedIn). 5-7: Broad targeting. 1-4: Hard to target digitally |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: EUR 3K+/mo ad spend + management. 5-7: EUR 1-3K/mo. 1-4: < EUR 1K/mo (not enough to learn) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Paid ads specialist on team. 5-7: Founder can manage with tools. 1-4: No one understands ads |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Low CPC in niche. 5-7: Average CPC. 1-4: Expensive CPC (> EUR 5), saturated auctions |
| Time-to-first-result | 8-10 typical. Results in days. Optimization = 2-4 weeks. Scale = 1-2 months. |

**Platform selection**:
- Google Ads: High search intent (transactional keywords), local services
- Meta Ads: B2C, visual products, retargeting, lookalike audiences
- LinkedIn Ads: B2B with EUR 5K+/mo budget (expensive but precise targeting)
- TikTok Ads: Gen Z, impulse purchases, app installs

**Best for**: E-commerce, SaaS with proven conversion funnel, Local services, Lead gen with proven landing page
**Budget range**: EUR 1K to EUR 50K+/mo (ad spend alone)
**Team requirement**: 3-5h/week minimum for management + creative refresh
**Tools**: Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn Campaign Manager

### 6. Community / Events (Online communities, webinars, meetups)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-Many, Organic (Warm)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: ICP values peer connection (founders, developers, niche professionals). 5-7: Some community interest. 1-4: ICP prefers solo consumption |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: Free (Slack/Discord). 5-7: EUR 200-500/mo (events, platform). 1-4: EUR 2K+/mo (conferences, sponsorships) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Community-minded team member. 5-7: Can dedicate 3h/week. 1-4: No community management capacity |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: No community exists for this niche. 5-7: Communities exist but fragmented. 1-4: Strong existing communities |
| Time-to-first-result | 3-5 typical. Engagement in weeks. Revenue impact = 3-6 months. Long game. |

**Best for**: Developer tools, SaaS platforms, Professional services, Vertical communities
**Budget range**: EUR 0 to EUR 3K/mo
**Team requirement**: 3-5h/week ongoing
**Tools**: Slack, Discord, Circle, Lu.ma, Zoom

### 7. Partnerships / Affiliates (Strategic alliances, referral programs)

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-One, Organic (Warm through partner)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: Clear partner ecosystem exists (integrations, adjacent services). 5-7: Some potential partners. 1-4: Isolated market, no natural partners |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: Revenue share model (no upfront). 5-7: EUR 500-2K/mo partner management. 1-4: EUR 5K+/mo partner programs |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Relationship builder on team. 5-7: Founder can manage 2-3 partners. 1-4: No BD capacity |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors don't partner. 5-7: Some partnerships exist. 1-4: Competitors have locked key partners |
| Time-to-first-result | 3-5 typical. First partnership = 1-3 months. Revenue impact = 3-6 months. |

**Best for**: SaaS with integrations, Professional services (cross-referral), Marketplace models
**Budget range**: EUR 0 (rev share) to EUR 5K/mo (partner programs)
**Team requirement**: 3-5h/week for relationship management
**Tools**: PartnerStack, impact.com, manual tracking

### 8. Product-Led Growth (PLG) — Free tier, freemium, viral loops

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-Many, Product (Organic/Warm)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: Users can self-serve and see value quickly. 5-7: Moderate onboarding needed. 1-4: Complex product requiring sales |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: Built into product (dev cost only). 5-7: Need product changes. 1-4: Major product rebuild needed |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Product team can implement. 5-7: Some dev resources. 1-4: No product team |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors don't offer free tier. 5-7: Free tiers exist. 1-4: Free alternatives abundant |
| Time-to-first-result | 4-6 typical. Depends on product changes. Adoption = 1-3 months after launch. |

**Requires**: Self-serve product with clear value demonstration
**Best for**: SaaS, Developer tools, Productivity apps
**Budget range**: Engineering cost (variable)
**Team requirement**: Product + engineering support
**Tools**: Product analytics (Mixpanel, Amplitude, PostHog)

---

### 9. ABM — Account-Based Marketing

**Hormozi Quadrant**: One-to-One, Paid (Warm to specific accounts)

| Dimension | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|---------------|
| ICP-fit | 8-10: Fewer than 500 target accounts, high ACV (EUR 50K+). 5-7: 500-2000 accounts, EUR 10-50K ACV. 1-4: Mass market, low ACV |
| Budget-fit | 8-10: EUR 5K+/mo (tools + content + ads). 5-7: EUR 2-5K/mo. 1-4: < EUR 2K/mo (not viable for ABM) |
| Team-capacity | 8-10: Dedicated ABM person or team. 5-7: Part-time focus. 1-4: No ABM experience |
| Competitive-gap | 8-10: Competitors not doing ABM. 5-7: Some ABM activity. 1-4: Competitors dominate key accounts |
| Time-to-first-result | 3-5 typical. First engagement = 1-2 months. Closed deals = 3-6+ months. |

**Best for**: Enterprise SaaS, Professional Services targeting large accounts, Complex B2B sales
**Budget range**: EUR 2K to EUR 20K+/mo
**Team requirement**: 10-15h/week dedicated
**Tools**: HubSpot ABM, Demandbase, 6sense, LinkedIn Ads

## Budget Tier Quick Guide

| Tier | Monthly Budget | Viable Channels |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Bootstrap (EUR 0-500) | Founder time only | Content/SEO, Social Organic, Cold Outreach (DIY) |
| Starter (EUR 500-2K) | Some tools | + Email Marketing, basic Paid Ads testing |
| Growth (EUR 2K-5K) | Tools + one hire | + Paid Ads (meaningful), Community, Partnerships |
| Scale (EUR 5K-15K) | Small team | + ABM, multi-channel Paid, dedicated outreach |
| Enterprise (EUR 15K+) | Full team | All channels viable, optimize allocation |
