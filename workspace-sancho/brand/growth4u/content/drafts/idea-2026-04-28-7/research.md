## Query 2: human orchestration AI agents B2B sales 2026 shift

**Sources:**
- https://www.allego.com/blog/human-ai-collaboration-the-new-frontier-in-b2b-sales/
- https://www.bcg.com/publications/2025/how-ai-agents-will-transform-b2b-sales
- https://www.abstraktmg.com/guide/guide-to-ai-lead-generation/future-of-ai-lead-generation/

**Key Findings:**
- AI agents augment human sales professionals, not fully replace them
- Agents handle: prospecting, lead scoring, data research, personalized outreach drafting, follow-ups
- Human role: strategy, ICP definition, reviewing outcomes, complex negotiations, trust-building
- HITL design: AI drafts, human reviews and adjusts before sending; AI identifies prospects, human prioritizes
- New required skills: agent orchestration, prompt engineering, data literacy, strategic creativity, ethical AI governance
- BCG: sales productivity up to +40%, sales cycle reduced up to -25% with autonomous agents
- Integration of AI agents is becoming a competitive necessity, not differentiator
