## Key findings

### AI Referral Traffic
- AI-sourced sessions grew **527%** YoY (Jan-May 2024 vs Jan-May 2025), from ~17K to ~107K sessions
- Generative AI traffic grew **796%** across 2.3B session analysis (Jan 2024 – Dec 2025)
- By 2025, AI accounts for ~0.18% of total web sessions — but converts **1.2x better** than organic search
- ChatGPT dominates (84.2% of AI traffic, 3.26x YoY growth) but Claude grew 12.8x and Copilot 25.2x
- Perplexity AI: +500% average sessions per user in 2024

### Zero-Click Searches
- 2024: **58.5–65%** of Google searches ended without a click to an external website
- 2025: **60–69%** zero-click globally; mobile reaches **75–77%**
- AI Overviews now appear on **30%+ of queries** (Q1 2026), up from 13% (March 2025)
- When AI Overviews appear, organic CTR drops **61%**

### GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- **50%+ of consumers** regularly use generative AI tools for online discovery
- GEO focuses on: factual accuracy, depth, E-E-A-T, structured data, conversational content
- Key difference vs SEO: GEO optimizes for **AI citation**, not just rankings
- Brand authority amplified when cited in AI-generated responses
- Without GEO strategy, brands remain invisible to AI-native discovery channels
