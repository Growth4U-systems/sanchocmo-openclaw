# Extract: Inter-American Dialogue — State of Remittance Industry 2025
**URL:** https://thedialogue.org/blogs/2025/04/the-state-of-the-remittance-industry-and-an-outlook-for-2025
**Accessed:** 2026-05-09 | **Rating:** A

## Key data extracted:
- Nearly $170 billion remitted to LAC in 2024 (small-dollar amounts)
- 80% from the United States
- 10 firms manage ~80% of US-LAC corridor flows
- Leading companies: Western Union, PayPal/Xoom, Viamericas, Remitly, MoneyGram, Ria, Intermex
- ~20 companies handle 90% of US outbound remittance market
- Remitly: grew from 14% to ~23% market share by 2024
- Viamericas: doubled market share from 5% to 10% in five years
- Digital and cash-originated transactions now almost evenly divided
- FelixPago (2022): remittances via WhatsApp, processing in ten-digit dollar range
- Outlook 2025: growth up to 5%, but US recession risk could dampen
- Expanding use of stablecoins/crypto as alternative currency options
- Notable: NO European fintechs (Revolut, Wise, N26) mentioned among top players in US-LAC corridor
