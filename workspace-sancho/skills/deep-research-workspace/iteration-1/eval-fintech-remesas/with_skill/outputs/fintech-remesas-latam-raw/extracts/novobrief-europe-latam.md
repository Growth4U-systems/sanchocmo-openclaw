# Extract: NovoBrief — Money Transfer Market Shift Europe-LatAm
**URL:** https://novobrief.com/the-money-transfer-market-shift-between-europe-and-latin-america/11792/
**Accessed:** 2026-05-09 | **Rating:** B

## Key data extracted:
- $174 billion sent home by Latin Americans in 2025
- Money transfer market: $12.7B (2025) → $44.9B (2035), 13.5% CAGR
- Migration from cash to digital apps is the main driver
- Traditional providers losing share
- Traditional advantage: physical locations, brand recognition, compliance systems
- Fintechs reducing importance of physical locations and brand via transparent pricing
- Operational knowledge (how transfers work day-to-day) is hardest to replicate
- Mexico: largest corridor at $61.8B received in 2025, but share declining
- Central America (Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador) growing quickly
- Peru: projected fastest-growing fintech market in South America through 2031
- Madrid as strategic base: EU regulatory passport, 4.2M Latin American residents in Spain (800K+ in Madrid)
- TucanPay case study: Madrid-based, €150K round, €2M valuation, ENISA-backed
- Targeting Peru, Mexico, Panama corridors
- Key challenge for new entrants: winning customers + regulatory approvals across jurisdictions + liquidity management across currencies
- South America: 35.7% remittances from US, 36.2% from Europe (key finding — Europe is bigger than US for South American remittances!)
