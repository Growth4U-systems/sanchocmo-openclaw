The number one reason people don't commit to a PM tool: *"My team won't actually use it."*

**Don't roll it out. Just start using it.**

4. **Let the tool prove itself** — when people see their work organized and visible, they ask to be added

You don't need a company-wide announcement. You don't need IT approval. You don't need a training session.
