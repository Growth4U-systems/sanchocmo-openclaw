## Body

Hi {{first_name}},

We built this because we lived the problem.

Our team was running a product launch across 4 time zones. We had tasks in spreadsheets, updates in Slack threads, deadlines in someone's head. The launch slipped by three weeks. Not because people weren't working — because nobody could see the full picture.

So we asked: **what if a project management tool was built for how teams actually work?** Not how consultants think they should work.

That meant:
- **No training manual required** — if you can drag a card, you can use it
- **Built for teams, not just managers** — everyone sees what matters to them
- **Flexible enough to not fight you** — your workflow, not ours

We're not trying to be everything. We're trying to be the tool your team actually opens every morning.

If you're still kicking the tires, that's totally fine. But if you want to see how it feels with real work — bring in a project you're working on right now.
