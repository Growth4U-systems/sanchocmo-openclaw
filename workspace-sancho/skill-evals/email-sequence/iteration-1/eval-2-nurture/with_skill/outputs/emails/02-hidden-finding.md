## Body

Hi {{first_name}},

Quick question: how does your team measure pipeline health?

If the answer is "deployment frequency," you're in good company. 78% of teams in our report said the same thing.

But here's what the data actually showed:

**Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR) is 3x more predictive of overall engineering velocity than deployment frequency.**

Teams obsessing over "how often we ship" were missing the bigger picture. The top performers focus on **how fast they recover when something breaks**.

Here's why it matters:

- High deployment frequency with slow recovery = outages pile up
- Moderate deployment frequency with fast recovery = steady, predictable shipping
- The top 15% have both — but they got there by fixing recovery *first*
