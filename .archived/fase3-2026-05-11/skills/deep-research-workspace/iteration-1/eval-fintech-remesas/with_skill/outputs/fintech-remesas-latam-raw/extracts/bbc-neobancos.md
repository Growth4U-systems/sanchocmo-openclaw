# Extract: BBC Mundo — Neobancos en América Latina
**URL:** https://www.bbc.com/mundo/articles/c939pwgz96go
**Accessed:** 2026-05-09 | **Rating:** B

## Key data extracted:
- Neobanks globally: ~312 entities listed in 2025
- 1+ billion combined customers (Simon Kucher & Partners)
- 36 new neobanks appeared between 2022-2023
- Latin America: 23% of world's neobanks
- N26 exited Brazil in November 2023 (high competition + operational challenges)
- Revolut now offers 20+ products (including travel, lounges, crypto)
- Nu (Nubank): reached 100M customers across Brazil, Mexico, Colombia
- Key challenge: profitability — most neobanks still unprofitable
- Digital payments in LatAm: 48% of eCommerce value, 30% in-store (heading into 2025)
- Cash's share of in-store: fallen to 25%, continuing to decline
- BBVA cited as example of traditional bank with strong digital transformation in LatAm
- LatAm neobanks largely domestic (Nequí, Yape, Daviplata, PeiGo, BEN)
- Regulatory flexibility post-2008 crisis enabled neobank explosion (started in UK)
