# Extract: Wise — Profile & LatAm Operations
**Sources:**

**Accessed:** 2026-05-09

## Key data extracted:

### Company-level:
- Founded: 2011 (as TransferWise)
- Customers: 16+ million across 160+ countries
- Cross-border volume: £145.2 billion
- Underlying income: £1.4 billion
- Listed on LSE (WISE:LSE)

### Business model:
- NOT a bank — fintech company
- Bypasses SWIFT by holding local bank accounts in each country
- Uses mid-market exchange rate with NO markup
- Fee structure: fixed fee + variable (0.33% to ~2% depending on corridor)
- ACH cheapest funding method; credit card most expensive

### LatAm coverage:
- Supports transfers TO Colombia (COP), Mexico (MXN), Brazil (BRL), and others
- Fees to Mexico: ~$8-12 on $1,000 (0.8-1.2%)
- Fees to Colombia: 0.5-1.5% range
- Delivery: 1-2 business days for bank transfers
- Limitation: NO cash pickup (major gap for LatAm remittance market)
- Partnership with N26 for international transfers (including Brazil, Mexico)

### Competitive positioning:
- vs banks: dramatically cheaper (0.5-1.5% vs 8-12% total cost via bank wire)
- vs Western Union/MoneyGram: competitive on price, weaker on cash distribution
- Europe generates majority of revenue (per Statista)
- Does NOT have banking licenses in LatAm countries
- Operates cross-border FROM established markets, doesn't operate locally IN LatAm
